Faculty Information

The faculty of Engineering and Information Technology (FEIT) is a faculty within the Southern University College (Southern UC). It was established on August 12, 2012 after Southern College (SC) was upgraded to Southern UC. The history reaches back to two decades ago, when the Department of Computer Science was first established in 1991, followed by the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering in 2004. These two departments were then integrated and renamed as the School of Engineering and Information Technology (SEIT) in March 2006.

Faculty Messages

Outcome Based Education Training on 18 June 2020

2020/7/7

On 18 June 2020, Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology has conducted Outcome Based Education (OBE) Training. The instructor is Assoc. Prof. Dr. Sim Hiew Moi. The participants are lecturers from Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology. The purpose of this training is to introduce the concept of OBE and how to apply it in teaching and learning. In the training, the instructor has introduced the concept of OBE, the terms of Programme Educational Objectives (PEO), Programme Learning Outcome (PLO), and Course Learning Outcome(CLO), the relationship among the terms and the way of implementing OBE in teaching and documentation. The instructor also shows the examples of using OBE in setting exam question and assignment, how to match PEO with PLO and CLO, how to use OBE to assess continual quality improvement and demonstrates an OBE system to lecturers. FEIT lecturers have gained a lot of information and knowledge from the OBE training and it helps them to cope with the teaching and documentation in the future.

Brain-Computer Interface Southern Region Semi Final Contest on 30th September 2019

2019/10/3

Braintech together with TusStar Malaysia organized the Southern Region Semi Final for the BCI Contest using Mind-Controlled Drone on 30th September 2019 that was hosted by Southern University College. 8 teams which came from south and east-coast universities such as Universiti Malaysia Pahang, Southern University College and Universiti Teknologi Malaysia competed for the southern region. There were 4 teams of Southern University College students participated in this contest. The final contest is expected to be held on the end of November and will be held in Kuala Lumpur.

FEIT Lecturers attended Introduction to Data Science Training on 21st & 22nd September 2019

2019/10/3

On 21st & 22nd September 2019, Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology lecturers went to two days Introduction to Data Science Training. The training was conducted by UCAN TECHNOLOGIES SDN. BHD. The speaker was Dr. Lau Cher Han. He is a chief data scientist, keynote speaker and consultant in data science, machine learning and big data. In these two days, the FEIT lecturers learned about Data Science Overview, Data Science Processes, Intro to Python in Data Science and Application of Data Science. After attending the training, the FEIT lecturers understand the requirements within an industry data driven team and know how to apply data science methodologies in real world use cases.
